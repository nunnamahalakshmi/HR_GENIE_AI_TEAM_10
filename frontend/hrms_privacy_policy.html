Section 1

Introduction

Welcome to the Human Resource Management System (HRMS) Portal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you access and use our platform.

By accessing or using HRMS Portal, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

This system is intended for authorised employees and HR personnel only. Unauthorised access is strictly prohibited and may result in legal proceedings.

Section 2

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), and assigned role
  • Employment Information: Name, employee ID, department, designation, joining date
  • Payroll Information: Salary details, bank account details, tax deductions, PF/ESI
  • Attendance Data: Login/logout times, leave records, shift schedules
  • System Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, session duration
  • Documents: ID proof, address proof, offer letters and employment contracts
Data Storage
Microsoft SharePoint — India Region
Access Control
Role-based (HR Admin / Employee)
Encryption
HTTPS / TLS 1.2+ in transit
Retention Period
5 years after employment ends
Section 3

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and provide secure access to the portal
  • To manage payroll processing, attendance records, and leave management
  • To generate HR reports, analytics, and statutory compliance documents
  • To send system notifications, alerts, and HR communications
  • To detect and prevent unauthorised access or fraudulent activity
  • To comply with applicable labour laws and government regulations in India

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Section 4

Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within the Organisation: HR Admins may access employee data as required for their role
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Auditors: Internal or external auditors for compliance verification
  • Service Providers: Microsoft (SharePoint, Power Automate) under strict data processing agreements
Section 5

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures:

  • All data transmissions are protected using HTTPS / TLS encrypted connections
  • Passwords are stored using industry-standard encryption — never in plain text
  • Access is role-restricted — users can only view data relevant to their role
  • Session timeouts are enforced to prevent unauthorised access
  • Regular security reviews and access audits are performed
Section 6

Your Rights

As a user of HRMS Portal, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
Section 7

Cookies & Session Data

HRMS Portal uses browser session storage and minimal cookies for functional purposes only:

  • To maintain your authenticated login session while using the system
  • To remember your role and redirect you to the correct dashboard
  • To store temporary UI preferences for your current session

We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies.

Section 8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When significant changes are made, users will be notified via the HRMS dashboard and the effective date will be updated.

Continued use of HRMS Portal after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
